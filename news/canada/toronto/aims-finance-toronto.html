    New AIMS AI trading system attracts growing interest in Canada

    17 hours ago|
    Duration 3:30
    AIMS Finance has introduced a new AI-driven trading platform designed to analyze global markets in real time. Industry experts say the technology could open new opportunities for Canadian investors interested in automated financial tools.

    Social Sharing

    Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming industries around the world — and financial markets are no exception. A growing number of Canadian investors are now exploring automated trading platforms designed to analyze global markets and execute trades in real time.




    One platform attracting significant attention is AIMS — the Artificial Intelligence Melissa System, a technology company that has developed an AI-powered trading system designed to simplify investing and generate consistent market opportunities.

    According to the company, more than 1,000 Canadians have already joined the platform, using its automated tools to participate in global financial markets.

    A New Generation of AI-Powered Trading

    The AIMS system was developed by entrepreneur and trader Melissa Brown, who gained recognition in the trading industry for her performance in global markets.

    Together with AI engineer Mark Eddisson, Brown invested more than $3 million in research and development to build the system that now powers the AIMS platform.

    The result is an advanced algorithm designed to process enormous volumes of market data in real time.

    The platform continuously analyzes:

    • global stock and cryptocurrency exchanges
    • financial news and economic indicators
    • geopolitical and political events
    • historical trading patterns and technical charts

    By combining these sources of information, the AIMS AI system identifies market opportunities and executes trades automatically.

    Speed and Scale: 1,000 Trades Per Minute

    One of the key technological advantages of the platform is its high-frequency trading capability.

    According to the company, AIMS can execute more than 1,000 micro-trades per minute across multiple exchanges worldwide.

    This allows the system to distribute capital across thousands of positions, reducing exposure to individual market movements and enabling a diversified trading strategy.

    Supporters of the technology say this approach allows the system to capture small market fluctuations that would be impossible for manual traders to identify in time.

    Millions Generated Through the System

    The company reports that over $44 million was generated in 2025 by Melissa Brown and her trading partners using the AIMS system.

    How It Works: Three Simple Steps

    1. Open an account — The client creates an account on the AIMS platform and completes verification.
    2. Deposit funds — Funds are transferred to the trading account, where the AI system begins managing them.
    3. Withdraw after one month — After approximately 30 days, the client can withdraw both the initial deposit and the returns generated by the system.

    Example: A user deposits $500 into their account. One month later, they withdraw $14,500 — their initial investment plus profits generated by the AIMS trading algorithm.

    While individual results vary, the platform was designed to make advanced trading technology accessible even to people with no prior trading experience.

    Many users say the biggest advantage is automation — the AI performs the market analysis and trade execution while users monitor performance through their dashboard.

    Personal Investment Support

    Another feature that distinguishes the platform is its personal investment manager program.

    Each user is assigned a manager who helps guide them through the activation process and explains how the system operates.

    Managers also help users:

    • set realistic income targets
    • understand risk management strategies
    • monitor performance and account activity

    The company says this personal support helps new investors feel more confident when entering financial markets.

    Designed for Simplicity

    AIMS promotes a simple onboarding process designed to make participation accessible.

    The typical process includes:

    • Selecting a monthly income target
    • Checking whether places in the program are available
    • Completing a short registration
    • Reviewing the activation plan with a manager
    • Activating the system and monitoring trading activity

    According to the company, the entire registration process takes less than five minutes.

    The Future of AI in Finance

    Artificial intelligence is already widely used by hedge funds, banks and institutional traders. Systems capable of analyzing global data streams in real time are increasingly becoming a standard part of financial infrastructure.

    Platforms like AIMS aim to bring similar technology to individual investors.

    While financial experts remind investors that all trading carries risk, many believe that AI-powered analysis will continue to play a growing role in the future of financial markets.

    For Canadians exploring automated investment technology, platforms like AIMS represent a new generation of tools designed to combine advanced AI, global market analysis and user-friendly automation.
